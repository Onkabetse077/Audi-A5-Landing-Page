Sporty and fit for the whole family

Designed to amaze and to drive

Wide air inlets and chrome trim give the front of this sporty five-door vehicle its edge. At the sides, mid-heavy sills underscore its sportiness and pronounced wheel arches emphasise the optional 20-inch alloy wheels. Horizontal trim in matt aluminium silver on the diffuser and trapezoid tailpipe trims reinforce the wideness of the rear. In addition, the Audi A5 Sportback can be customised with the optional advanced and S line equipment lines.

Powerful dynamics

Driving dynamics-supporting technologies such as the optional dynamic steering combined with the powerful engines ensure a convincing driving experience.

Compromises? Not necessary

Thanks to its large interior, the luggage compartment and rear of the Audi A5 Sportback offer plenty of space for leisure and family. The spacious feel is visually underscored by the horizontal design elements in the interior, the user-friendly centre console, door rail trims in gloss black, and the high-quality materials. Customise your vehicle further with the optional interior design selection and the optional S line equipment line. The state-of-the-art infotainment platform and the optional ambient lighting package plus lend the interior of the Audi A5 Sportback its progressive elegance.
